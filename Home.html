About our club

Lytham is located on the Fydle coast between Preston and Blackpool. It hosts many sports team not least Lytham Town FC. Lytham Town play in the West Lancashire League division one whilst their reserve team play in the West Lancashire League reserve division two

There are over sixty players registered for the club many of whom are under eighteen and primarily play in the resrerve team aiming on making the standard to get into the prestigious first team.
